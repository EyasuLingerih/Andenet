Software Development Agreement

Contract Number: ADA-2025-001

Date:

Client: Andenet Ayele Electromechanical and General Construction PLC
Developer: Eyasu Lingerih

This Software Development Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date signed below, by and between Andenet Ayele Electromechanical and General Construction PLC ("Client") and Eyasu Lingerih ("Developer").

1. Project Scope

The Developer agrees to design, develop, and deliver the following software solutions for the Client:

  • Front Page Website - A professional, responsive website showcasing the Client's business
  • In-House Workflow Web Application - A custom web-based application for internal workflow management

2. Project Timeline

2.1 The project shall be completed within two (2) months from the date of this Agreement.

2.2 The Developer will provide regular progress updates to the Client throughout the development process.

3. Payment Terms

3.1 The total project cost is 150,000 ETB (One Hundred Fifty Thousand Ethiopian Birr).

3.2 Payment shall be made in three (3) installments as follows:

Payment Stage Percentage Amount (ETB) Due Date
First Payment (Upfront) 30% 45,000 Upon signing this Agreement
Second Payment (Milestone) 30% 45,000 Upon completion of design and 50% functionality
Third Payment (Final) 40% 60,000 Upon project completion and delivery
Total Project Cost 150,000

3.3 All payments shall be made via bank transfer or other mutually agreed payment method.

4. Maintenance and Support

4.1 The Developer shall provide free maintenance and technical support for six (6) months following the final delivery and acceptance of the project.

4.2 Free maintenance includes:

  • Bug fixes and error corrections
  • Minor updates and adjustments
  • Technical support and troubleshooting

4.3 After the six (6) month free maintenance period, the parties may enter into a separate paid maintenance contract under mutually agreed terms and pricing.

4.4 Major feature additions or significant modifications requested during or after the maintenance period shall be subject to separate negotiation and pricing.

5. Intellectual Property Rights

5.1 Upon final payment, all intellectual property rights, including source code, designs, and documentation, shall be transferred to the Client.

5.2 The Developer retains the right to use the project as a portfolio piece unless otherwise agreed.

6. Confidentiality

6.1 Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality regarding proprietary information, business data, and sensitive materials shared during the project.

7. Warranties and Liabilities

7.1 The Developer warrants that the delivered software will be free from major defects and will function as specified during the free maintenance period.

7.2 The Developer's liability shall be limited to the total amount paid under this Agreement.

8. Termination

8.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with written notice if the other party breaches any material term and fails to remedy such breach within fourteen (14) days of written notice.

8.2 In the event of termination, the Client shall pay for all work completed up to the date of termination.

9. General Provisions

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Ethiopia.

9.2 Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through good faith negotiation or, if necessary, through appropriate legal channels in Ethiopia.

9.3 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions and understandings.
